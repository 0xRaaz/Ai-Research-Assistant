How it works

From PDF to answers
in under 30 seconds

01
Upload your paper
Drop a PDF or paste an arXiv URL. ResearchAI extracts and cleans the full text, preserving section structure and references.
02
Automatic chunking + embedding
The paper is split into semantic chunks and embedded into a vector store using ChromaDB — indexed and ready in seconds.
03
Ask anything
Type your question in plain English. Your query is embedded, matched against the index, and the most relevant passages retrieved.
04
Get cited, grounded answers
Gemini-2.5 Flash synthesises the retrieved passages into a clear answer — every claim linked back to its exact section in the paper.
